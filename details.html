MISSION

"To build a community or let me say a global village or family of innovators if not dreamers who are not judged or scaled by their capability of doing the same thing but by doing what they know best and are good at it Okay here is a story that we hope will help you understand our mission and what we believe in, also what we intend to do: Say we have an elephant, fish and a monkey. You take these and put them in a system that measures their competence and success. The system then asks them to swim as their test of performance. You ca guess what happens what the outcome is. The fish is the victor in such a story and the other two the monkey and the elephant can be judged as incapable of anything by the system because of this on act. Some might believe that thy can't do anything and thy fight to do what others are doing, but if the system was to do the measurement by making them climb a tree we all know that the odds will favor the monkey. Why then are we forced to believe that we can't when we can? Why do we let a system define what we can and cannot do? Be a monkey if you ary one don't let anyone judge you by what a fish can do because you are not a fish. I say do what you know and do it well and your best. Your best is always enough. The reason why most of us don't grow is that we want to do what individual X is doing be who you are and fight hard to be the best version of the fish, monkey or elephant you are. Our mission then is to help you become the person you want to be. Nurture you and support you in any way we can. Open doors for you. Introduce you to a family of dreamers who will not judge you but will help you too to build your dream."
